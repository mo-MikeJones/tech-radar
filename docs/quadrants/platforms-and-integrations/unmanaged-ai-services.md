## Summary
The use of public AI services, APIs or tools that have not been approved, assessed for security and data handling, or integrated with standard access controls and monitoring.

## Why we're interested
This practice was initially common as teams moved quickly to experiment with AI. It poses unacceptable security and compliance risks at scale.

## Current status
Not recommended. All AI services must be assessed and approved before use. Approved services are listed in the AI Tools Registry.

## Evidence & feedback
Several data handling policy violations identified relating to use of unapproved AI services. Moving to managed, approved services resolved these issues.

## Known risks / concerns
Unmanaged AI services may process sensitive code, data or credentials without adequate privacy or security controls. Compliance violations are a material risk.

## Recommended audience
This practice is not recommended for any audience. Use only AI services listed in the approved AI Tools Registry.

## Next steps
All teams to audit current AI tool usage against the approved registry. Report unapproved services to the security team. Remove unapproved integrations within 30 days.
