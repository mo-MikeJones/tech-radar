## Summary
A structured programme where volunteer teams explore cutting-edge AI capabilities, document their findings and share learnings back to the broader engineering organisation.

## Why we're interested
AI capabilities are evolving rapidly. Frontier teams allow the organisation to stay ahead of the curve while managing risk through structured experimentation.

## Current status
First cohort of frontier teams active. Experiments underway across several practice areas. Monthly sharing sessions established.

## Evidence & feedback
High engagement from participating teams. Strong signal on several promising capabilities. Monthly showcases well attended and positively reviewed.

## Known risks / concerns
Frontier work can be distracting if not well scoped. Experiments must have clear hypotheses and defined success criteria to be valuable.

## Recommended audience
Senior engineers and technical leads interested in advancing AI practices. Teams with bandwidth for structured experimentation.

## Next steps
Publish findings from the first cohort. Open applications for the second cohort. Develop a clearer framework for defining and evaluating experiments.

## Owner
CTO Office / AI Platform Team
