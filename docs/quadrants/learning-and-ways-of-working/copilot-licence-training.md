## Summary
Structured onboarding and training for all engineers receiving GitHub Copilot licences, ensuring they understand both the capabilities and the responsible use guidelines.

## Why we're interested
Licences without training lead to suboptimal and potentially unsafe usage. Structured onboarding maximises return on the licence investment.

## Current status
Mandatory onboarding training live. All new licence holders complete training before activation. Refresher content under development.

## Evidence & feedback
Engineers who complete training report higher satisfaction and better outcomes with Copilot. Reduction in misuse incidents since training became mandatory.

## Known risks / concerns
Training content must be kept current as Copilot capabilities evolve. Risk of engineers treating training as a tick-box exercise rather than genuine learning.

## Recommended audience
All engineers receiving a Copilot licence. Engineering managers tracking team compliance.

## Next steps
Develop advanced training modules for experienced Copilot users. Add training completion to licence renewal requirements.
