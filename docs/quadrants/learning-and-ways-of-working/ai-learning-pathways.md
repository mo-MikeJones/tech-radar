## Summary
Structured learning programmes to help engineers at all levels develop effective AI-augmented development skills, from basic prompt engineering to advanced agentic workflows.

## Why we're interested
Unguided adoption of AI tools leads to inconsistent outcomes. Structured learning ensures engineers get the most from AI tooling safely and effectively.

## Current status
Core learning paths defined. Beginner and intermediate paths available. Advanced agentic development path in development.

## Evidence & feedback
Engineers who complete structured learning report significantly better outcomes from AI tooling. Positive feedback on clarity and relevance of content.

## Known risks / concerns
Learning content requires continuous updates as AI capabilities evolve. Risk of content becoming stale if not actively maintained.

## Recommended audience
All engineers. Particularly valuable for those new to AI-assisted development.

## Next steps
Complete the advanced agentic development learning path. Integrate learning pathway completion into engineering onboarding.
