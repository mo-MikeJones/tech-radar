## Summary
Documented workflows and best practices for working effectively with AI coding agents, including how to structure tasks, review outputs and maintain quality standards.

## Why we're interested
As AI agents become more capable, clear guidance on effective usage patterns is essential to ensure safe and productive adoption across teams.

## Current status
Initial playbooks developed and available on the internal developer portal. Being refined through team feedback.

## Evidence & feedback
Teams using the playbooks report more consistent, higher quality outcomes from AI agents. Particularly valuable for engineers new to agentic development.

## Known risks / concerns
Playbooks can become outdated quickly as AI tooling evolves. Requires ongoing maintenance and community contribution to stay relevant.

## Recommended audience
All engineers adopting AI coding agents. Engineering leads responsible for team AI practices.

## Next steps
Publish playbooks on the internal developer portal. Set up a quarterly review cycle to keep content current as tooling evolves.

Developer Experience Team
