## Summary
A curated library of shared AI agent definitions, prompt templates and custom instructions that teams can adopt and adapt for their own workflows.

## Why we're interested
Reduces duplication across teams and accelerates adoption of effective AI practices. Enables community-driven improvement of AI tooling configurations.

## Current status
Library in early stages. A small number of high-quality shared definitions available. Growing adoption across teams.

## Evidence & feedback
Teams using shared libraries report faster setup time and more consistent AI output quality compared to starting from scratch.

## Known risks / concerns
Library quality depends on community contribution and curation. Stale or low-quality agents can mislead new adopters.

## Recommended audience
All engineering teams beginning or scaling their AI tooling adoption.

## Next steps
Launch an internal developer portal for discovering and contributing agent definitions. Implement a lightweight review process for submissions.
