## Summary
Snowflake Cortex is a suite of AI and ML capabilities built directly into the Snowflake data platform, enabling LLM inference and AI-powered analytics over data in Snowflake.

## Why we're interested
Enables AI capabilities close to where our data lives, reducing data movement and latency for AI-powered analytics and data-driven agent workflows.

## Current status
Under evaluation by the data platform team. Initial experiments with LLM inference and AI-powered SQL generation are promising.

## Evidence & feedback
Strong integration with existing Snowflake workflows. LLM inference on data in Snowflake removes the need to extract data for AI processing. Governance and access control models are mature.

## Known risks / concerns
Cortex capabilities vary by Snowflake region and tier. Cost implications of LLM inference at scale within Snowflake need careful modelling before broad adoption.

## Recommended audience
Data engineers and analytics engineers adding AI capabilities to data pipelines. Data product teams exploring AI-powered analytics.

## Next steps
Complete evaluation of LLM inference use cases. Assess cost model. Publish guidance for teams wanting to use Cortex capabilities.
