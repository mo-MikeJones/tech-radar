## Summary
Integration of ServiceNow's AI capabilities (Now Intelligence and Now Assist) with engineering workflows, including AI-powered incident management and knowledge automation.

## Why we're interested
ServiceNow is a key operational platform. AI integrations can significantly reduce manual effort in incident response and knowledge creation processes.

## Current status
Initial evaluation underway. ServiceNow Now Assist capabilities being assessed against specific engineering workflow use cases.

## Evidence & feedback
Promising capabilities for incident summarisation and knowledge article generation. Integration with existing ITSM processes is straightforward. Still early in evaluation.

## Known risks / concerns
AI in incident management requires careful validation. Incorrect AI recommendations could delay incident resolution. Human oversight remains essential throughout.

## Recommended audience
Platform engineers and SREs involved in incident management. Engineering leads responsible for knowledge management.

## Next steps
Define high-value use cases. Run pilots on incident summarisation and knowledge article automation. Measure impact before broader rollout.

## Owner
Platform Engineering / ITSM Team
